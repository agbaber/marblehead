About the site

This vote matters. So before voting, I wanted to understand two things: how we got here, and what each possible outcome would actually mean. Is this about cuts we could make, or costs rising faster than revenue? I honestly don't know. The site is where I'm working that out.

About AI usage

An AI assistant (Claude) handles research, analysis, and the site's digital infrastructure. I regularly remind it to stay as neutral as possible, and I own and operate everything it produces. Every number traces to a primary source, but I don't re-verify each one against the original document myself. Errors are possible. When I find one, or a reader catches one, the page gets updated.
