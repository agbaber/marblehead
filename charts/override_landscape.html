Where Does Marblehead's Override Land?

Every operating override of $2M or more since FY2020, across all Massachusetts municipalities. Marblehead's proposed FY2027 override ($8.47M) marked for context.

  • 65 override elections of $2M+ since FY2020. 28 passed, 34 failed, 3 split
  • At the $5M-10M range where Marblehead's ask sits, 8 of 15 passed (53%)
  • Marblehead's prior attempts ($2.5M in FY2024, $3.1M in FY2023) both failed, at smaller amounts than the current ask
  • Comparable recent wins: Braintree $8M, Belmont $8.4M, Milton $8.8M. Comparable recent losses: Bridgewater $8M, Malden $8.2M
