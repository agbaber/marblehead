Where Does Marblehead's Override Land?

Every operating override of $2M or more since FY2020, across all Massachusetts municipalities.

  • Operating overrides only. Debt exclusions (capital projects, school buildings) not included
  • 65 override elections of $2M+ since FY2020. 28 passed, 34 failed, 3 split
  • Marblehead's three nested tiers are $9M, $12M, and $15M. Tier 1 falls in the $5M-10M range (53% pass rate); Tiers 2 and 3 fall in $10M+ (58%)
  • Marblehead's prior attempts ($2.5M in FY2024, $3.1M in FY2023) both failed at smaller amounts
