Annual new levy revenue (~$2.3M at 3%/yr) compared to annual increases in healthcare, pension, and salary costs (~$5.1M combined). These are projections from FY26 base values using Finance Committee growth rates, not actual year-by-year data. Pension base (~$5.4M) is estimated.
