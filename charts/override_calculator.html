What the Override Costs You

Additional monthly cost by home assessed value. Median Marblehead home: $1,010,100.

At full phase-in (Year 3, FY29)

Option $750K $1M $1.25M $1.5M
Tier 1 ($9M) $56/mo $75/mo $94/mo $113/mo
Tier 2 ($12M) $75/mo $100/mo $125/mo $150/mo
Tier 3 ($15M) $94/mo $125/mo $156/mo $188/mo

Tier amounts from Town Administrator's override presentation (Marblehead Independent). Years 1 and 2 are lower as the override phases in.

Tiers are nested: Tier 2 includes Tier 1, Tier 3 includes Tiers 1 and 2. Highest passing tier sets the amount.

Trash/recycling is a separate ballot question and is not included above. You'll pay for it either way: as a levy increase (~$19/mo on a $1M home) or as a flat fee (~$22/mo per household) if the override fails.
