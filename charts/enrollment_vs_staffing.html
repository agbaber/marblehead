School Enrollment and Education Staffing

Marblehead Public Schools, FY2001–FY2024. From audited Annual Comprehensive Financial Reports.

Student enrollment

Education full-time equivalent employees

Student enrollment peaked at 3,327 (FY14) and has declined 21% to 2,617 (FY24). Education FTE ranged from 400 (FY01) to 537 (FY24). The FY23 jump from 483 to 537 education FTE is unexplained in public records and may reflect federal ESSER-funded positions or a change in reporting methodology. Students per education FTE dropped from 6.8 (FY07) to 4.9 (FY24).

Enrollment from ACFR "Demographic and Economic Statistics" tables. May include or exclude charter school students depending on year.

Education FTE from ACFR "Full-time Equivalent Town Employees by Function," education row. FTE counts part-time employees as fractions. Includes teachers, paraprofessionals, administrators, custodial, and all other school staff.

The declining enrollment / flat staffing pattern is common across Massachusetts and is partly driven by special education mandates (IEP-required staffing), out-of-district placements ($4.6M in FY26 school budget), and federal requirements that do not scale with enrollment.
