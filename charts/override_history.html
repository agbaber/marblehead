Notes and sources

Override vote data from the Massachusetts Department of Revenue, Division of Local Services Municipal Databank. The peer set matches the 14 towns tracked in peer_override_history.csv: Arlington, Brookline, Cohasset, Framingham, Hingham, Lexington, Marblehead, Melrose, Natick, Needham, Stoneham, Swampscott, Wellesley, Winchester.

One Marblehead row (FY2025, "Fix Clerk Error", 0 votes, $0) is excluded as a clerical correction, not an actual ballot question.

Vote margins are calculated as yes votes minus no votes. Some fiscal years had multiple ballot questions (e.g. Marblehead FY2005 had six questions, Needham FY1997 had ten). Each question is shown as a separate dot.
