Health Insurance Spending and Staffing

Marblehead, FY2006-FY2027. From audited Annual Comprehensive Financial Reports and Finance Committee reports.

Health insurance spending grew from $8.0M to $16.8M over 21 years. Full-time equivalent headcount ranged from 655 to 709. Active members on the insurance plan ranged from 642 to 748. Neither staffing measure shows growth proportional to the spending increase.

Health insurance from Annual Comprehensive Financial Report (ACFR) budget schedules (FY06-FY13) and Finance Committee reports (FY14-FY25). FY20 not available. FY26-FY27 proposed budget (dashed). Includes active employee health insurance, Medicare supplement, and Medicare reimbursement.

Full-time equivalent counts part-time employees as fractions (e.g., 20 hrs/wk = 0.5). Insurance plan active membership counts all benefits-eligible employees at 20+ hours/week as whole members. These are two different ways of measuring staffing; neither is complete on its own.

Town joined the state Group Insurance Commission in FY2012 per audited ACFR. FY13 insurance membership jump (642 to 748) not explained in public records.
