Sources and methodology

Average single-family tax bill: MA DOR DLS, FY2026. Per-capita income: DOR Municipal Databank, FY2027. The ratio uses per-capita income (not median household income) because it is available for all communities from one source. This is a different denominator than the four-town comparison on the Rate and Bill page (which uses ACS median household income). Both confirm the same relative ranking.
