Where Your Tax Dollars Go

Enter your home value to see how your FY26 property tax bill flows to town and school spending. Toggle tiers to see your override cost and what it funds.

Don't know it? Look it up on the town's tax portal. Defaults to the town's average single-family value.

    Your tax bill is calculated at the FY26 residential rate of $8.59 per $1,000 of assessed value. Spending shares are proportional: the general fund has no line-item earmarking, so each dollar of property tax is allocated across categories in the same ratio as the overall FY26 budget.

    Town department totals from the FY26 Proposed Budget. School total from the FY26 School Committee approved budget. Override line items and no-override cuts from the Town Administrator's April 8, 2026 override presentation. Override cost-per-household scaled from average single-family assessed value of $1,291,507 using the same method as the override cost calculator.
