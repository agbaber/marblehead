Marblehead's tax rate is 28% lower than Swampscott's, and its median home value is 57% higher. The median Marblehead tax bill is ~$1,100/yr higher in absolute terms. On the same $750K home, Marblehead's annual tax is $2,557 lower. Swampscott provides ~30% more square footage per dollar of home value. Swampscott median tax bill source: Ownwell (less authoritative than DOR).

Property taxes are paid per property, not per person. A family of four pays the same as a single resident.
