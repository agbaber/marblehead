Marblehead's Prop 2½ voting record

Since 1988, Marblehead has voted on 70 Proposition 2½ questions: operating overrides and debt exclusions. Here is every one, with the dollar amount, the department, and the vote count.

What is an override vs. a debt exclusion?

Operating override permanently raises the tax levy ceiling. The added capacity stays in the base forever.

Debt exclusion temporarily raises the levy ceiling for the life of a specific bond. When the bond is paid off, that capacity drops back out.
