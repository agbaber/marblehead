                                    School Committee Workshop
                                     October 19, 2021 at 7:00pm
                                 Marblehead High School Auditorium


Members present -Sarah Gold, Meagan Taylor, David Harris, Emily Barron
Participated Virtually- Sarah Fox
Other Attendees- Dr. John Buckey, Superintendent
                  Michelle Cresta, Assistant Superintendent of Finance and Operations
                  Legal Firm Representative, Colby Brunt


1. Call to order
    a. Ms. Gold called the meeting to order at 7:02 pm

2. Stoneman, Chandler & Miller LLP
    a. Presentation by Colby Brunt
       Ms. Colby Brunt from Stoneman, Chandler and Miller LLP introduced herself and
       provided some background information on the firm.

       Each committee member took turns welcoming Ms. Brunt and introduced themselves.

       After a presentation, the committee took turns asking a series of questions pertaining to areas of
       expertise, training offerings and the approach to ongoing policy review.

3. Legal Representation Discussion
   Ms. Taylor made a motioned to approve Stoneman, Chandler & Miller, LLP as our new legal counsel
   which was seconded by Mr. Harris. After a brief discussion, the motion passes, 5-0.

4. Adjournment
   Ms. Gold adjourned the meeting at 7:57pm
