                                        9/15/2021 at 6:00pm
                                 Marblehead High School Auditorium


Members present -Sarah Gold, Meagan Taylor, David Harris, Emily Barron
Participated Virtually- Sarah Fox
Other Attendees- Dr. John Buckey,Superintendent
                  Michelle Cresta, Assistant Superintendent of Finance and Operations
                  Legal Firm Representatives


   I.      Call to order
           Ms. Gold called the meeting to order at 6:00pm

   II.     Legal Representation Presentations
           a.Valerio Dominello & Hillman, LLC
           Current legal representative since 2010, Ms. Elizabeth Valerio introduced herself to
           committee members she had not yet had the opportunity to meet. She provided some
           background information of the firm.

           Each committee member took turns welcoming Ms. Valerio and introducing themselves.

           After a presentation, the committee took turns asking a series of questions pertaining to areas
           of expertise, new training offerings and the firms approach to ongoing policy review in the
           time of COVID.

           Ms. Valerio and the firm were commended for their quick response time over the years.

           b.Stoneman, Chandler & Miller LLP
           Representatives Rebecca Bryant and Tom Costello introduced themselves and provided some
           background information of the firm. Ms. Bryant informed the committee that she would be
           sitting in for Ms. Colby Brunt that evening.

           Each committee member took turns welcoming the representatives and introducing
           themselves.

           After a presentation, the committee took turns asking a series of questions pertaining to areas
           of expertise, training offerings focusing on open meeting law and the approach to ongoing
           policy review in the time of COVID.

           The representatives noted that their firm consisted of two dedicated special education lawyers
           with an area of the firm focusing solely on litigation.

   III.    Legal Representation Deliberation
           After the presentations, a discussion regarding the differences amongst firms took place. The
           size of the firms and firm fees were further discussed. Although no formal vote was taken, it
           was decided that the committee would like to invite Ms. Brunt from Stoneman, Chandler &
           Miller LLP to present prior to making a final decision.

          Ms. Gold adjourned the meeting at 8:02pm
