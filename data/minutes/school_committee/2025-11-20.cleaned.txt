MHS Roof Contract Presentation and Discussion (00:24:00–00:54:00)

a. Bid results for Marblehead High School roof and HVAC project:
    ●​ Subcommittee member Brian Serafin attended the meeting in person. Members Marc Leibman and Ralph
       Wallace participated remotely.
    Six general contractor bids received; subcommittee recommended Homer Contracting Inc. of Arlington,
    Massachusetts at $8,970,000, $2,120,000 under budget of $11,090,000
    • Subcommittee voted unanimously not to pursue liquid applied roofing alternate, which would have added
    over $2 million to project cost

    • Project required to accept low bid (Homer Contracting)
b. Liquid applied roofing alternate discussion:
    • Mark Liebman explained extensive material damage requires removal, repair, and PVC installation before
    liquid application, making it more expensive rather than cost-effective
    • Three roofing subcontractor bids varied widely: Capeway bid $1.8 million extra, Greenwood bid $983,000
    extra, Gibson Roofing proposed $157,000 credit
c. Procurement and project details:
    • Eight general contractors pre-qualified with six bidding; approximately 70% of contract value is HVAC
    equipment, 30% roofing. Details provided by Ralph Wallace
    • Project goal is substantial completion by October 2026
d. Subcommittee continuation and oversight:
    • Subcommittee will remain active through project completion with all five community members continuing
    • Mark Liebman offered frequent site visits during roofing installation and quarterly maintenance oversight
    meetings after completion
    • Jennifer Schaeffner requested regular updates to full School Committee and suggested formalizing ongoing
    maintenance oversight through Facilities subcommittee
e. Financial implications of $2 million savings:
    • Michael Pfifferling reported savings cannot be used for other purposes and must reduce debt exclusion
    • Jennifer Schaeffner requested clarification on how savings will flow back to taxpayers
f. A motion was made by Henry Gwazda and seconded by Jennifer Schaeffner to award the contract for the general
contractor for the Marblehead High School roof HVAC project to Homer Contracting Inc. of Arlington,
Massachusetts, in the amount of $8,970,000. The motion passed 4–0, with votes in favor: H. Gwazda, J.
Schaeffner, M. Clucas, K. Schmeckpeper; and votes against: none.
