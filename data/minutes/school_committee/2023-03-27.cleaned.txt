I. Initial Business
a. Call to Order
    Ms. Fox called the meeting to order at 6:03pm. Committee member Mathers was not present.

II. FY24 Budget Vote
    Ms. Fox asked for a motion to approve the FY24 reduced services budget in the amount of $44,837,273.
    The motion was moved by Meagan Taylor and seconded by Ms. Gold.

    Prior to voting Ms. Meagan Taylor inquired about the increase from FY23 the Town will support for FY24.
    Ms. Fox confirmed an amount of $855K. A roll call vote was taken and the motion carries, 4-0.

    Ms. Alison Taylor, Ms. Meagan Taylor, Ms. Gold, Ms. Fox-all yes.

    Ms. Fox asked for a motion to approve the FY24 level services budget in the amount of $45,971,790 which
    is contingent up a proposition 2 ½ override. The motion was moved by Ms. Gold and seconded by Ms.
    Meagan Taylor.

    Ms. Taylor confirmed that the numbers included 1) contractual obligations, 2) utilities costs and 3) curriculum
    associated costs to which Ms. Fox confirmed that it did along with any existing out of district special
    education associated costs.

    Prior to a roll call vote being taken, Mr. Mathers joined the meeting. A roll call vote was taken and the motion
    carries, 5-0.

    Ms. Alison Taylor, Ms. Meagan Taylor, Ms. Gold, Ms. Fox, Mr. Mathers-5-0.

 III. Closing Business
