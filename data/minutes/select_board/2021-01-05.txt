                             BOARD OF SELECTMEN

                                      MINUTES

                                    January 5, 2021

Board met in session at 9:30 a.m. via ZOOM Conferencing:

                               Jackie Belf-Becker, Chair
                                     David Depew
                                  M. C. Moses Grader
                                     James E. Nye

                            Jason Silva, Town Administrator

The Chair called the meeting of Tuesday, January 5, 2021 to order. The following roll
call was taken: Mr. Depew, present; Mr. Grader, present; Mr. Nye, present; Mrs. Belf-
Becker, present.

APPOINTMENT. Interim Treasurer/Tax Collector. Motion made and seconded to
appoint Cami Ianarelli Interim Treasure/Collector of Taxes. Moved by Mr. Grader,
seconded by Mr. Nye. On a polled vote the Board voted as follows: Mr. Grader, in
favor; Mr. Nye, in favor; Mr. Depew, in favor; Mrs. Belf-Becker, in favor.

APPOINTMENT. Interim Town Accountant. Motion made and seconded to appoint
Michelle Cresta Interim Town Accountant. Moved by Mr. Nye, seconded by Mr. Grader.
On a polled vote the Board voted as follows: Mr. Grader, in favor; Mr. Nye, in favor;
Mr. Depew, in favor; Mrs. Belf-Becker, in favor.

CONTRACT. Financial Support Services. Motion made and seconded that the Board
approve the contract for financial support services with Eric A. Kinsherf, CPA and that
the Board authorize the Chair to execute same. Moved by Mr. Nye, seconded by Mr.
Grader. On a polled vote the Board voted as follows: Mr. Grader, in favor; Mr. Nye, in
favor; Mr. Depew, in favor; Mrs. Belf-Becker, in favor.

CONDOLENCES. Motion made and seconded to send a letter of condolence to the
family of Bill James, former Cemetery Superintendent and MHS football coach. Moved
by Mr. Nye, seconded by Mr. Grader. On a polled vote the Board voted as follows: Mr.
Grader, in favor, Mr. Nye, in favor; Mr. Depew, in favor; Mrs. Belf-Becker, in favor.

APPRECIATION. The Board expressed thanks to Yeimi Colon, former Assistant
Accounting Director, for her professional behavior and stepping up to assist the Town
temporarily.
