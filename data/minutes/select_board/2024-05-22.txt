BELL RINGING. July 4th. Motion made and seconded to request that church bells
throughout the Town be rung on Wednesday, July 4, 2024 at the usual holiday hours:
7:30 a.m. to 8:00 a.m. / 12:00 noon to 12:30 p.m. / 6:00 p.m. to 6:30 p.m. All in favor.

CONTRACTS. The Town Administrator reviewed the following contracts with the
Board; the following votes were taken:

Marblehead Redd’s Pond Accessibility Improvements Plan. Motion made and
seconded to award a contract for the development of a Marblehead Redd’s Pond
Accessibility Improvements Plan to Pare Corporation of Foxborough, MA in the amount
of Eighty-Seven thousand five hundred ($87,500.00) and authorize the Chair to sign the
contract on behalf of the Board. All in favor.

Rail Trail Improvements. Salem line to West Shore Drive. Motion made and
seconded to award a contract for the development of design and construction documents
for Rail Trail Improvements from the Salem Line to West Shore Drive to Toole Design
of Boston, MA in the amount of three hundred seventy-eight thousand six Hundred and
eighty-four dollars. ($378,684.00) and authorize the Chair to sign the contract on behalf
of the Board. All in favor.

Preliminary Design of Rail Trail. Swampscott to Smith Street. Motion made and
seconded to award a contract for the project start up to Preliminary Design of Rail Trail
improvements from the Swampscott Line to Smith Street to Toole Design of Boston,
MA in the amount Not to Exceed One hundred sixty-two thousand dollars ($162,000.00)
and authorize the Chair to sign the contract on behalf of the Board. All in favor.

LEASE. North Shore Medical Center. Alterations. Motion made and seconded to
approve the request from Diana Lombard, Real Estate Manager, Salem Hospital, to
perform necessary work to upgrade the elevator at 1 Widger Road all in accordance with
Section 17, Alteration and Installation of Equipment as stated on the Lease between the
Town of Marblehead and North Shore Medical Center, Inc., dated March 6, 2023. All in
favor.

the request from the Rev. C. Clyde Elledge, St. Andrews Church, for a one-day liquor
license for Saturday, June 29, 2024, at 135 Lafayette Street, 6:00 p.m.- 9:00 pm subject to
the following conditions:
    • Delivery of and receipt by the Licensing Authority of the required fee ($50).
    • Delivery of and receipt by the Licensing Authority of Proof that the alcohol will
        be purchased from an authorized source.

ABBOT HALL. Permission to Use. Marblehead Museum. Motion made and
seconded to approve the request from Lauren McCormack, Executive Director,
Marblehead Museum, to use Abbot Hall on Monday, October 14, 2024 for 2024
Indigenous Peoples Day event from 10:00 am – 2:00 pm subject to the usual rules,
regulations, fees and receipt of the required Certificate of Insurance and to waive the
rental fee for this event. All in favor.

PUBLIC COMMENT. None.
