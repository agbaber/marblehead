                              BOARD OF SELECTMEN

                                       MINUTES

                                  November 20, 2018

Board met in session at 8:50 a.m. in the Selectmen’s Meeting Room at Abbot Hall
Present (constituting a quorum):

                                Jackie Belf-Becker, Chair
                                 Harry C. Christensen, Jr.
                                   M. C. Moses Grader
                                     Judith R. Jacobi

                            Jason Silva, Town Administrator

LICENSING. 1 Day Liquor License. Lumiere Lauren. Motion made and seconded
to approve the request from Charlotte Daher de Garcia, Lumiere Lauren, for a 1 Day
Liquor License on Tuesday, November 27, 2018 from 6:00 p.m. – 9:00 p.m. at 123
Pleasant Street, subject to the following:
       1. Delivery of and receipt by the Licensing Authority of the required fee ($50.)
       2. Delivery of and receipt by the Licensing Authority of Proof that the alcohol
          will be purchased from an authorized source.
       3. Proof that the applicant can receive proper delivery; provide proper storage and
          disposal of all alcoholic beverages purchased all in accordance with the
          requirements of G. L. c. 138.
Alcohol shall be purchased from Kappy’s in Everett. On a polled vote the Board
voted as follows: Mr. Grader, in favor; Mr. Christensen, in favor; Mrs. Jacobi, in
favor; Mrs. Belf-Becker, in favor.
