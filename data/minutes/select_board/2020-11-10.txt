ABBOT HALL. Permission to Use. Festival of Arts. Motion made and seconded to
approve the request from the Marblehead Festival of Arts to use Abbot Hall, Upper
Grounds, on Saturday, December 5, 2020 from 7:00 a.m. — 4:00 p.m., for a small Artisans
Marketplace (10:00 a.m. — 3:00 p.m.) subject to approval from the Board of Heath,
receipt of the required fees, forms, Certificate of Liability and adherence to all COVID-
19 health and safety protocols in place at that time. Weather date is Sunday, December 6,
2020. Moved by Mr. Nye, seconded by Mr. Depew. On a polled vote the Board voted as
follows: Mr. Depew, in favor; Mr. Nye, in favor; Mrs. Belf-Becker, in favor.

Motion made and seconded to adjourn at 1:33 p.m. Moved by Mr. Nyc, seconded by Mr.
