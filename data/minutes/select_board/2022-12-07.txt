CHRISTMAS TREE BURNING / REMOVAL. Christmas Trees will be picked up at
the curbside from Tuesday, December 27, 2022 through Friday, January 6, 2023 and will
be placed at Riverhead Beach for the community bonfire on Friday evening January 6,
2023. The Christmas Tree pile will be set alight at 6:00 p.m. Residents are invited to
attend this community event on January 6, 2023. After January 6, 2023 residents should
bring trees to the town’s transfer station for proper disposal. All trees left curbside for
pick up from December 27th until January 6th must have all lights, ornaments, and stands
removed and not be in plastic bags. Any tree not meeting these requirements will not be
picked up. In addition, holiday wreaths, roping and garland will not be picked up.

PUBLIC COMMENT. The following public comments were taken:

     Street design.

TOWN ADMINSTATOR UDPATE. The Town Administrator updated the Board on
the following:
    • River Rhine – painting auction. The Historical Commission reported that
        BonhamsSkinner auction has been completed and the River Rhine painting sold
        for $850, more than BonhamsSkinner had expected. The Select Board had
        authorized putting the painting up for auction at the September 8, 2021, meeting.
    • Sustainability Coordinator. Town Administrator said this is an ongoing
        discussion and if it becomes part of the budget, it will be presented to the Board
        then.
