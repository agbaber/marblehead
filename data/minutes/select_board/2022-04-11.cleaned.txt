                              BOARD OF SELECTMEN

Board met in session at 7:00 p.m. at the Mary A. Alley Building, Widger Road
Present (constituting a quorum):

                               Jackie Belf-Becker, Chair
                                  M. C. Moses Grader
                                     James E. Nye
                                   Erin M. Noonan

The Chair called the meeting of April 11, 2022 to order. The following roll call was
taken: Mrs. Noonan, present; Mr. Grader, present; Mrs. Singer, present, participating
remotely; Mr. Nye, present; Mrs. Belf-Becker, present.

The Board attended the Finance Committee Warrant Hearing.

Motion made and seconded to adjourn at 10:40 p.m. Moved by Mr. Nye, seconded by
Mr. Grader. On a polled vote the Board voted as follows: Mrs. Noonan, in favor; Mr.
Grader, in favor; Mr. Nye, in favor; Mrs. Belf-Becker, in favor.
