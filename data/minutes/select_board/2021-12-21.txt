                              BOARD OF SELECTMEN

                                       MINUTES

                                  December 21, 2021

Board met in session at 11:45 a.m. via ZOOM Conferencing
Present (constituting a quorum):

                                Jackie Belf-Becker, Chair
                                   M. C. Moses Grader
                                      James E. Nye
                                    Erin M. Noonan
                                     Alexa J. Singer

The Chair called the meeting of December 21, 2021 to order. The following roll call was
taken: Mrs. Noonan, present; Mrs. Singer, present; Mr. Nye, present; Mrs. Belf-Becker,
present.

LEASE AGREEMENT. MT7 Tractor. Steve Poulos, Finance Director and Rob
Dever, Director of Public Works, appeared before the Board as it relates to a lease
agreement for an MT7 Tractor. Mr. Grader joined the meeting. Motion made and
seconded to enter into the Lease with Option to Purchase Agreement Number TE-2266,
for a New Trackless MT7 Tractor, between All American Investment Group, LLC
(Lessor) and the Town of Marblehead as presented and authorize the Chair to sign on
behalf of the Board. Moved by Mr. Nye, seconded by Mrs. Singer. On a polled vote the
Board voted as follows: Mrs. Noonan, in favor; Mr. Nye, in favor; Mrs. Singer, in favor;
Mr. Grader, in favor. Mrs. Belf-Becker, in favor.

Motion made and seconded to adjourn at 11:50 a.m. Moved by Mr. Nye, seconded by
Mr. Grader. On a polled vote the Board voted as follows: Mrs. Noonan, in favor; Mr.
Nye, in favor; Mrs. Singer, in favor; Mr. Grader, in favor. Mrs. Belf-Becker, in favor.
