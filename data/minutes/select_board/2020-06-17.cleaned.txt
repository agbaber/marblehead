OUTDOOR SEATING. The following applications were reviewed by the Board for
outdoor seating:
The Little Store, 9 Green Street. Matthew Gateman appeared before the Board seeking
permission for outdoor seating and to have his Carry-In Beverage License approved
outside as well. After review, motion made and seconded, Pursuant to Executive Order
COVID-19, No. 35, issued by Governor Baker on June 1, 2020, and notwithstanding the
provisions of Chapter 40A of the General Laws, or any special permit, variance or other
approvals thereunder, or any general or special law to the contrary, the Board of
Selectmen for the Town of Marblehead approves the application for outdoor seating
submitted by Matthew Gateman, The Little Store, 9 Green Street, provided that the

operation remains in substantial compliance with the documents, plans and statements
contained within said application and submitted therewith, which documents are
incorporated herein by reference. This approval is freely revocable upon the
determination of the Health Department, Chief of Police, Board of Health, or their
designees that the operation of the outdoor dining area is negatively impacting public
health and safety. Further, this approval shall automatically be rescinded on November 1,
2020 or upon the recission of Executive Order COVID-19, No. 35, whichever shall come
first and is subject to final approval by the Town Administrator, Police and Fire. Trash
pick-up shall be required 7 days a week between the hours of 7:00 am and 5:00 pm.
Moved by Mrs. Jacobi, seconded by Mr. Grader. On a polled vote the Board voted as
follows: Mr. Nye, in favor; Mrs. Jacobi, in favor; Mr. Grader, in favor; Mr. Depew, in
favor; Mrs. Belf-Becker, in favor.
