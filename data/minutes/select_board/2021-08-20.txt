                              BOARD OF SELECTMEN

                                      MINUTES

                                    August 20, 2021

Board met in session at 12:00 p.m. at Abbot Public Library, Conference Room
Present (constituting a quorum):

                               Jackie Belf-Becker, Chair
                                  M. C. Moses Grader
                                     James E. Nye
                                   Erin M. Noonan
                                    Alexa J. Singer

                            Jason Silva, Town Administrator

The Chair called the meeting of August 20, 2021 to order. The following roll call was
taken: Mrs. Noonan, present; Mrs. Singer, present; Mr. Grader, present; Mr. Nye,
present; Mrs. Belf-Becker, present.

The Board met in open session for the purpose of holding a retreat to review the role and
goals of the Board. Steve Poulos, Finance Director, presented a financial overview of the
Town and Jason Silva, Town Administrator gave a presentation on the accomplishments,
goals and objectives for the Town. Members then asked questions, offered feedback and
engaged in discussion.

3:15 p.m. Mr. Nye excused himself from the meeting.

No votes were taken.

Motion made and seconded to adjourn at 3:30 p.m. Moved by Mr. Grader, seconded by
Mrs. Singer. On a polled vote the Board voted as follows: Mrs. Noonan, in favor; Mrs.
Singer, in favor; Mr. Grader, in favor; Mrs. Belf-Becker, in favor.
