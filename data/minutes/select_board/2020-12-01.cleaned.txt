                             BOARD OF SELECTMEN

                                      MINUTES

                                   December 1, 2020

Board met in session at 10:00 a.m. via ZOOM Conferencing:

                                Jackie Belf-Becker, Chair
                                      David Depew
                                   M. C. Moses Grader
                                      James E. Nye

                           Jason Silva, Town Administrator

The Chair called the meeting of Tuesday, December 1, 2020 to order. The following roll
call was taken: Mr. Grader, present; Mr. Nye, present; Mrs. Belf-Becker, present.

MINUTES. Motion made and seconded to approve the minutes of November 4, 2020,
November 10, 2020, November 18, 2020 and November 23, 2020. Moved by Mr. Nye,
seconded by Mr. Grader. On a polled vote the Board voted as follows: Mr. Nye, in
favor; Mr. Grader, in favor; Mrs. Belf-Becker, in favor.

Mr. Depew joined the meeting.

APPOINTMENT. Building Commissioner. Robert Scott appeared before the Board.
Motion made and seconded to appoint Robert Scott as Building Commissioner for the
Town of Marblehead. Moved by Mr. Nye, seconded by Mr. Grader. On a polled vote the
Board voted as follows: Mr. Nye, in favor; Mr. Grader, in favor; Mr. Depew, in favor;
Mrs. Belf-Becker, in favor.

Motion made and seconded to adjourn at 10:11 a.m. Moved by Mr. Grader, seconded by
Mr. Depew. On a polled vote the Board voted as follows: Mr. Nye, in favor; Mr. Grader,
in favor; Mr. Depew, in favor; Mrs. Belf-Becker, in favor.
