MINUTES. Motion made and seconded to approve the minutes from January 22, 2020.
All in favor.

CARNIVAL. Marblehead High School. Devereux Beach. The following members of
the MHS Student Council appeared before the Board: Tess Keaney, Carly Schauer and
Tess Whalen. Motion made and seconded to approve the request from Tess Keaney to
hold the annual MHS Student Council Carnival at Devereux Beach May 7 – 10, 2020
subject to approval from the Recreation and Parks Department, receipt of the required
Certificate of Insurance and all necessary permits. All in favor.

GIFT TO THE TOWN. President Herbert Hoovers Letter. Judy Anderson appeared
before the Board to present a 300th anniversary letter from President Herbert Hoover to
the citizens of Marblehead in 1929. This letter was among many archival items
belonging to Marbleheader Joseph Robinson (1902-1982). Motion made and seconded to
accept as a gift to the Town a 300th anniversary letter from President Herbert Hoover to
the citizens of Marblehead in 1929, and place in the care and custody of the Historic
Commission. All in favor.

       Proof that the applicant can receive proper delivery; provide proper

LICENSING. One Day Liquor License. Star of Sea. Motion made and seconded to
approve the request from Robert Mitchell, Facilities Manager, Our Lady, Star of the Sea

Parish, for a 1 Day Liquor License on Friday, May 29, 2020 from 6:00 p.m. – 11:00 p.m.
at 85 Atlantic Avenue subject to the following:
    1. Delivery of and receipt by the Licensing Authority of the required fee ($50.)

PERMISSION. Black Cat 10/20 Miler. B&S Fitness. Motion made and seconded to
approve the request from Brandi Dion, B&S Fitness Companies, to hold the 9th Annual
Black Cat 10/20 Miler on Saturday, April 4, 2020 subject to approval of the Marblehead
Police, receipt of the required Certificate of Insurance naming the Town of Marblehead
as additionally insured and police details. Approval is also subject to weather and road
conditions. No permanent markings shall be made on the streets and any temporary
markings shall be removed at the conclusion of the event. All in favor.

PERMISSION. Dory Run. High5EM. Motion made and seconded to approve the
request from Ashley Steeves, High5EM, to hold the Dory Run on Sunday, May 24, 2020
subject to approval of the Marblehead Police, receipt of the required Certificate of
Insurance naming the Town of Marblehead as additionally insured and police details.
No permanent markings shall be made on the streets and any temporary markings shall be
removed at the conclusion of the event. All in favor.

CONTRACT. July 4th Fireworks Display. Motion made and seconded to award the
contract for the July 4, 2020 Fireworks Display to Atlas Pyro Vision Entertainment
Group, Inc., of Jaffrey, New Hampshire and authorize the Chair to sign a contract on
behalf of the Board. All in favor.

PARKING RESTRICTIONS. Abbot Hall. Election Day. Motion made and
seconded to approve the following parking restrictions from Monday, February 24
through Friday, February 28, 2020, for the purpose of facilitating early voting at Abbot
Hall: “VOTER PARKING ONLY” at 188 Washington Street beginning at the driveway
entrance and continue to Washington Square. Parking will only be restricted as needed
during regular business hours, Monday, Tuesday and Thursday, 8:00 a.m. – 5:00 p.m.,
Wednesday 8:00 a.m. 6:00 p.m. and Friday 8:00 a.m. – 12:30 p.m., and will be returned
to general use as soon as it is deemed the restrictions are no longer needed. All in favor.

CONTRACT. Dental Insurance. Extend. Motion made and seconded to extend the
contract for voluntary dental insurance coverage between the Town of Marblehead and
Altus Dental Insurance Co. Inc., until 6/30/2022 and authorize the Chair to sign the
contract extension on behalf of the Board. All in favor.

CONTRACT. Ferry Lane Sewall Project. Motion made and seconded to award the
contract for the Ferry Lane Seawall project to T. Ford Co. of Georgetown, MA in the
amount of One hundred and eighty-three thousand nine hundred and thirty-five Dollars
($183,935.00) and authorize the Chair to sign the contract on behalf of the Board. All in
favor.

CONTRACT. Fort Restoration, Fort Sewall. Motion made and seconded to award the
contract for the Fort Restoration at Fort Sewall Phase II to Ace Restoration Co. Inc. of
Medford, MA in the amount of One hundred and twenty-three thousand five hundred
Dollars ($123,500.00) which includes the base bid and alternate and authorize the Chair
to sign the contract on behalf of the Board. All in favor.

Motion made and seconded to adjourn at 7:50 p.m. All in favor.
