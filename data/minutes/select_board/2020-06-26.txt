2020 ANNUAL TOWN MEETING. Warrant Articles. The Board reviewed articles
they have sponsored on the 2020 Annual Town Warrant and the following votes were
taken:
Article 9. Lease of Equipment. Motion made and seconded to revise the previously
approved vote to support Article 9 Lease of Equipment in the amount of $130,596.00.
Moved by Mrs. Jacobi, seconded by Mr. Grader. On a polled vote the Board voted as
follows: Mr. Nye, in favor; Mrs. Jacobi, in favor; Mr. Grader, in favor; Mr. Depew, in
favor; Mrs. Belf-Becker, in favor.
Article 28. Compensation Town Officers. Motion made and seconded to revise the
previously approved vote to support the Town Clerk’s salary at $82,755.92. Moved by
Mrs. Jacobi, seconded by Mr. Nye. On a polled vote the Board voted as follows: Mr.
Nye, in favor; Mrs. Jacobi, in favor; Mr. Grader, in favor; Mr. Depew, in favor; Mrs.
Belf-Becker, in favor.

OUTDOOR SEATING. OUTDOOR SEATING. The following applications were
reviewed by the Board for outdoor seating.
La Sirena Cafe. 146 Washington Street. Teresa Sirignano appeared before the Board
seeking permission for outdoor seating. After review, motion made and seconded,



June 26, 2020                              86
pursuant to Executive Order COVID-19, No. 35, issued by Governor Baker on June 1,
2020, and notwithstanding the provisions of Chapter 40A of the General Laws, or any
special permit, variance or other approvals thereunder, or any general or special law to
the contrary, the Board of Selectmen for the Town of Marblehead approves the
application for outdoor seating submitted by Teresa Sirignano for La Sirena Cafe, located
at 146 Washington Street in Marblehead, Massachusetts, provided that the operation
remains in substantial compliance with the documents, plans and statements contained
within said application and submitted therewith, which documents are incorporated
herein by reference. This approval is freely revocable upon the determination of the
Health Department, Chief of Police, Board of Health, or their designees that the operation
of the outdoor dining area is negatively impacting public health and safety. Further, this
approval shall automatically be rescinded on November 1, 2020 or upon the recission of
Executive Order COVID-19, No. 35, whichever shall come first and is subject to final
approval by the Town Administrator, Police and Fire.. Moved by Mrs. Jacobi, seconded
by Mr. Nye. On a polled vote the Board voted as follows: Mr. Nye, in favor; Mrs.
Jacobi, in favor; Mr. Grader, in favor; Mr. Depew, in favor; Mrs. Belf-Becker, in favor.

Motion made and seconded, pursuant to Executive Order COVID-19, No. 35, issued by
Governor Baker on June 1, 2020, and notwithstanding the provisions of Chapter 138 of
the General Laws, or any or any general or special law or local bylaw, rule, regulation to
the contrary, and the Town’s Carry In Beverage Policy, the Board of Selectmen, as the
Local Licensing Authority pursuant to G.L. c. 138 for the Town of Marblehead shall
permit the application for outdoor seating submitted by Loretta Lang for Maddie’s Sail
Loft located at 15 State Street in Marblehead, Massachusetts, provided that the operation
remains in substantial compliance with the documents, plans and statements contained
within said application and submitted therewith which documents are incorporated herein
by reference. This approval is freely revocable upon the determination of the Health
Department, Chief of Police, Board of Health, or their designees that the operation of the
outdoor dining area is negatively impacting public health and safety. Further, this
approval shall automatically be rescinded on November 1, 2020 or upon the recission of
Executive Order COVID-19, No. 35, whichever shall come first and is subject to final
approval by the Town Administrator, Police and Fire. Moved by Mrs. Jacobi, seconded
by Mr. Nye. On a polled vote the Board voted as follows: Mr. Nye, in favor; Mrs.
Jacobi, in favor; Mr. Grader, in favor; Mr. Depew, in favor; Mrs. Belf-Becker, in favor.
