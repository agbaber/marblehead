POLICE CHIEF. Interviews. Appointment. The Chair read a statement from
Shumeane Bendford, one of 3 finalists for Police Chief, withdrawing his name from
consideration, choosing to remain in his current role. Chief Benford thanked the Town
for a professional and comprehensive process and for the opportunity to be a finalist. The
Chair noted that this is the end of a lengthy process led by the Screening Committee
including extensive public engagement. Dennis King and Scott Richards were
interviewed separately before the Board. After completion of the interviews motion
made and seconded to place both names into nomination. Moved by Mr. Nye, seconded
by Mr. Grader. On a polled vote the Board voted as follows: Mrs. Belf-Becker, Dennis
King; Mr. Grader, Dennis King; Mr. Nye, Dennis King. Motion that the Marblehead
Board of Selectmen appoint Dennis King as the Marblehead Chief of Police subject to
completion and approval of a background check, successful completion of a physical,
completing all pre-employment requirements and agreement to terms of a contract.
Moved by Mr. Nye, seconded by Mr. Grader. On a polled vote the Board voted as
follows: Mr. Grader, in favor; Mr. Nye, in favor; Mrs. Belf-Becker, in favor.

FIREWORKS COMMITTEE. Alexander Falk, Chairman, Fireworks Committee,
appeared before to recommend that the annual July 4th Fireworks Display and Harbor
Illumination be cancelled this year. This recommendation comes after thorough review
of Governor Bakers reopening procedures and regulations regarding large scale
gatherings, safety, health and security concerns discussed by relevant Town Department
Heads, as well as discussions with the commercial fireworks providers.
