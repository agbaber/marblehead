BUDGETS. The following Departments appeared before the Board to present their
FY2022 budgets:

Police Department. Police Chief Robert Picariello appeared before the Board to present
his proposed budgets for FY 2022. Motion made and seconded to approve $4,094,438.00
for Salaries, $182,390.00 for Expense, $5,000.00 for Indemnification of Officers, for a
total operating budget of $4,280,369.00. Moved by Mr. Nye, seconded by Mr. Grader.
On a polled vote the Board voted as follows: Mr. Grader, in favor; Mr. Nye, in favor;
Mrs. Belf-Becker, in favor.

Animal Inspector. Motion made and seconded to approve $2,400.00 for Salaries, for a
