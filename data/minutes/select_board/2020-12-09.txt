FORT SEWALL OVERSIGHT COMMITTEE. Renovation Update. Larry Sands,
Chairman, Fort Sewall Oversight Committee and Rebecca Cutting, Town Planner,
appeared before the Selectmen to present a progress report on the Fort Sewall Renovation
Project. The Fort is slated to reopen in early Spring. The Committee is planning a
lecture on Fort History next spring by Rick Detwiller as well as implementing the Fort
Ranger Program and publishing a Fort Sewall booklet in celebration of the 100th
Anniversary of the Fort’s return to the Town of Marblehead. A thank you event to honor
the donors will be held as well. Marblehead Arts Association is planning an Art Exhibit
and Auction for late summer. To view the presentation visit www.marblehead.org .

CHRISTMAS TREE PICKUP. Notice was received from Robert Dever, Director of
Public Works, that Christmas Trees will be picked up at the curbside from Monday,
December 28, 2020 through Friday, January 15, 2021 After January 15, 2021 residents
should bring trees to the town’s transfer station for proper disposal. All trees left
curbside for pick up or brought to the transfer station must have all lights, ornaments, and
stands removed and cannot be in plastic bags. Any tree not meeting these requirements
will not be picked up. In addition, holiday wreaths, roping and garland will not be picked
up.
