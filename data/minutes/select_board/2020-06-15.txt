The Chair called the meeting of Monday, June 15, 2020 to order. The following roll call
was taken: Mr. Grader, present; Mrs. Jacobi, present; Mr. Nye, present; Mrs. Belf-
Becker, present.

MARBLHEAD MUNICIPAL EMPLOYEES UNION. Attorney Nikki Harberger
Decter, MMEU Counsel and Terri Tauro, Union President appeared before the Board to
discuss the public records request submitted by MMEU to the Town for financial
documents relating to FY21 budgets and any proposed cuts in staffing and services.
Marc Miller, Special Town Counsel, responded that the Town is in the process of
finalizing the budget and compiling the data requested and will make it available to the
union once it is completed.

OUTDOOR SEATING. The following applications were reviewed by the Board for
outdoor seating.
Gerry 5. 210 Beacon Street. Art Dodge appeared before the Board seeking permission
for outdoor seating. After review, motion made and seconded, pursuant to Executive
Order COVID-19, No. 35, issued by Governor Baker on June 1, 2020, and
notwithstanding the provisions of Chapter 40A of the General Laws, or any special
permit, variance or other approvals thereunder, or any general or special law to the
contrary, the Board of Selectmen for the Town of Marblehead approves the application
for outdoor seating submitted by Art Dodge for Gerry 5, located at 210 Beacon Street in
Marblehead, Massachusetts, provided that the operation remains in substantial
compliance with the documents, plans and statements contained within said application
and submitted therewith, which documents are incorporated herein by reference. This
approval is freely revocable upon the determination of the Health Department, Chief of
Police, Board of Health, or their designees that the operation of the outdoor dining area is
negatively impacting public health and safety. Further, this approval shall automatically
be rescinded on November 1, 2020 or upon the recission of Executive Order COVID-19,
No. 35, whichever shall come first and is subject to final approval by the Town
