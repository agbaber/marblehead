                             BOARD OF SELECTMEN

                                      MINUTES

                                    October 4, 2021

Board met in session at 7:00 p.m. via ZOOM Conferencing
Present (constituting a quorum):

                               Jackie Belf-Becker, Chair
                                  M. C. Moses Grader
                                   Erin M. Noonan
                                    Alexa J. Singer

                            Jason Silva, Town Administrator

The Chair called the meeting of October 4, 2021 to order. The following roll call was
taken: Mrs. Noonan, present; Mrs. Singer, present; Mr. Grader, present; Mrs. Belf-
Becker, present.

The Board met in session with the Finance Committee to review the budget process and
benchmark and comparative financial data, the new GFOA budget components and
reviewing examples of submittals, recommended modifications to budget schedule (start
budget sooner) and draft budget policies for the Town to consider. No votes were taken.

Motion made and seconded to adjourn at 8:47 p.m. Moved by Mr. Grader, seconded by
Mrs. Noonan. On a polled vote the Board voted as follows: Mrs. Singer, in favor; Mr.
Grader, in favor; Mrs. Noonan, in favor; Mrs. Belf-Becker, in favor.
