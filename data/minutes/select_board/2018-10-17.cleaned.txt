                              BOARD OF SELECTMEN

                                       MINUTES

                                    October 17, 2018

Board met in session at 8:30 a.m. in the Selectmen’s Meeting Room at Abbot Hall
Present (constituting a quorum):

                                Jackie Belf-Becker, Chair
                                 Harry C. Christensen, Jr.
                                   M. C. Moses Grader
                                     Judith R. Jacobi
                                      James E. Nye

                            Jason Silva, Town Administrator

CONTRACT. Abbot Hall Chimney Repair Project. Motion made and seconded to
award the contract for the Abbot Hall Chimney Project to International Chimney
Corporation of Buffalo, NY in the amount of eighty four thousand nine hundred dollars
($84,900.00) and authorize the Chair to sign the contract on behalf of the Board and to
use brick colored wrap on the chimney if necessary. All in favor.

102 GREEN STREET. Rescind Sale of Property. Motion made and seconded to
rescind the vote of October 3, 2018 to authorize the sale of the property at 102 Green
Street located in Marblehead to William Powers. All in favor.

102 GREEN STREET. Sale of Property. Motion made and seconded to authorize the
Town to execute the sale of the property at 102 Green Street located in Marblehead to
Glenn Rivera-Corona in the amount of four hundred and seventy three thousand eight
hundred dollars ($473,800.00) and authorize the Chair to sign on behalf of the Board. All
in favor.

CONGRATULATIONS. Motion made and seconded to send a letter of congratulations
to Jud Smith, who recently took first place in 2018 West Marine J/70 World Open
Championships held in Marblehead. All in favor.

Motion made and seconded to adjourn at 8:35 a.m. All in favor.

                                     Kyle A. Wiley
