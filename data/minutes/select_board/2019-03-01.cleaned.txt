BUDGETS. Alison Nieto, Finance Director, appeared before the Board to present
budgets with the following Departments:

Police Department. Police Chief Robert Picariello appeared before the Board to present
his proposed budgets for FY 2020. Motion made and seconded to approve $4,048,871.00
for Salaries, $182,390.00 for Expense, $5,000.00 for Indemnification of Officers, for a
total operating budget of $4,236,261.00. All in favor.
Animal Inspector. Motion made and seconded to approve $2,400.00 for Salaries, for a
total operating budget of $2,400.00. All in favor.

Fire Department. Fire Chief Jason Gilliland appeared before the Board to present his
proposed budget for FY 2020. Motion made and seconded to approve $3,885,738.00 for
Salaries, $189,600.00 for Expense, $5,000.00 for Fire Alarm Expense, for a total
operating budget of $4,080,338.00. 4 voted in favor, Mr. Christensen voted present.

Harbors and Waters Department. John Doub, Harbors & Waters Board, appeared
before the Board to present the Harbors & Waters Board proposed budget for FY 2020.
Motion made and seconded to approve $401,972.00 for Salaries, $407,991.00 for
Expense, $133,000.00 for Capital Outlays, for a total operating budget of $942,963.00.
All in favor.

Highway Department. Arthur Graves, Director of Public Works, appeared before the
Board to present his proposed budget for FY 2020. Motion made and seconded to
approve $922,922.00 for Salaries, $107,084.00 for Expense, $14,425.00 for
Rebuild/Maintain Streets and Sidewalks, $100,000.00 for Snow Removal, for a total
operating budget of be $1,144,431.00 All in favor.

Tree Department. Doug Gordon, Tree Warden, appeared before the Board to present
his proposed budget for FY 2020. Motion made and seconded to approve $247,898.00

for Salaries, $56,578.00 for Expense, for a total operating budget of $304,476.00. All in
favor.
