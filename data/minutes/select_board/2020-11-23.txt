                             BOARD OF SELECTMEN

                                      MINUTES

                                  November 23, 2020

Board met in session at 1:30 p.m. via ZOOM Conferencing:

                               Jackie Belf-Becker, Chair
                                     David Depew
                                  M. C. Moses Grader
                                     James E. Nye

                            Jason Silva, Town Administrator

The Chair called the meeting of Monday, November 23, 2020 to order. The following
roll call was taken: Mr. Depew, present; Mr. Grader, present; Mr. Nye, present; Mrs.
Belf-Becker, present.

EXECUTIVE SESSION. Motion made and seconded to go into Executive Session to
discuss strategy with respect to litigation, namely an appeal by Mango/Garthe of Building
Permit B-20-21 and for (alleged) failure to act in compliance with BCAB Order dated
August 21, 2019, Building Code Appeals Board (BCAB) Docket No. TBD, where an
open meeting may have a detrimental effect on the litigating position of the Town.
Moved by Mr. Grader, seconded by Mr. Depew. On a polled vote the Board voted as
follows: Mr. Grader, in favor; Mr. Depew, in favor, Mrs. Belf-Becker, in favor.

The Board adjourned to Executive Session at 1:35 p.m.
