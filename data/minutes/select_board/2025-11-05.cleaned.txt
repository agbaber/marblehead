The Chair called the meeting of November 5, 2025, to order.

PUBLIC COMMENT. None.

CONSENT AGENDA ITEMS, CONTRACTS. The Board reviewed contract requests.
Steve Cummings, Building Commissioner, and Brendan Callahan, Community
Development and Planning Director, appeared before the Board to present their contract
requests.

Motion made and seconded to approve the following Consent Agenda Items, contracts
with the Town, and authorize the Chair to sign on behalf of the Board:

   •   Change Order No. 1 for contract 26-04 Roof Replacements at the Mary Alley
       Building and the Jacobi Community Center between the Town and M.D.M.
       Engineering, Inc. in the amount of forty-four thousand eight hundred and sixty-
       four dollars and 49/100 ($44,864.49)

   •   Change Order No. 4 for contract MDPW 23-03 for Pavement Management
       Services between the Town and Apex Companies, LLC. Reducing the contract
       amount by one hundred five thousand and thirty-five dollars and 49/100 (-
       $105,035.49) with a contract extension until March 31, 2026

   •   Change Order No. 1 for contract 2024-053 Sidewalk Improvements between
       the Town and Richard F. D’Ambrosia, Inc. to extend the contract completion date
       to December 30, 2026

   •   Change Order No. 3, as outlined in the project synopsis, for Contract No. 2024-
       014-02 for the Design of the Rail Trail between the Town and Toole Design, to

        alter the tasks contained within the original proposal and extend the contract
        completion date to December 30, 2026

    •   Change Order No. 1 to extend Contract No. 2024-015 for the Design of the
        Rail Trail through December 30, 2026, and to update the scope of services as
        outlined in the project synopsis between the Town and Toole Design

All in favor.
