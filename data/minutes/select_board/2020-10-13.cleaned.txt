                             BOARD OF SELECTMEN

                                      MINUTES

                                   October 13, 2020

Board met in session at 10:00 a.m. via ZOOM Conferencing:

                               Jackie Belf-Becker, Chair
                                     David Depew
                                  M. C. Moses Grader
                                     James E. Nye

                           Jason Silva, Town Administrator

The Chair called the meeting of Tuesday, October 13, 2020 to order. The following roll
call was taken: Mr. Depew, present; Mr. Grader, present; Mr. Nye, present; Mrs. Belf-
Becker, present.

POLLING LOCATIONS. General Election. The Board reviewed the
recommendation from the Town Clerk as it relates to polling locations for the November
3, 2020 General Election. Motion made and seconded to approve, in accordance with
Section 11 of Chapter 115 of the Acts of 2020, and on recommendation from the Town
Clerk, the following changes to polling locations on November 3, 2020:
Precint 1 and 2:      Abbot Hall
                      188 Washington Street
Precint 3 and 4:      Marblehead Community Center
                      10 Humphrey Street
Precint 5 and 6:      Temple Emanu-El
                      393 Atlantic Avenue
Moved by Mr. Nye, seconded by Mr. Grader. On a polled vote the Board voted as
follows: Mr. Grader, in favor; Mr. Nye, in favor; Mr. Depew, in favor; Mrs. Belf-Becker,
in favor.

MINUTES. Motion made and seconded to approve the minutes of September 23, 2020.
Moved by Mr. Nye, seconded by Mr. Grader. On a polled vote the Board voted as
follows: Mr. Grader, in favor; Mr. Nye, in favor; Mr. Depew, in favor; Mrs. Belf-Becker,
in favor.

Motion made and seconded to adjourn at 10:06 a.m. Moved by Mr. Grader, seconded by
Mr. Nye. On a polled vote the Board voted as follows: Mr. Grader, in favor; Mr. Depew,
in favor; Mr. Nye, in favor; Mrs. Belf-Becker, in favor
