                              BOARD OF SELECTMEN

                                       MINUTES

                                   December 19, 2019

Board met in session at 6:15 p.m. at Abbot Hall, 188 Washington Street.
Town Administrators office.

Present (constituting a quorum):

                                Jackie Belf-Becker, Chair
                                 Harry C. Christensen, Jr.
                                     Judith R. Jacobi
                                   M. C. Moses Grader
                                      James E. Nye

                            Jason Silva, Town Administrator

EXECUTIVE SESSION. Motion made and seconded, pursuant to G.L. c. 30A sec
21(a)(3) that the Board move into executive session to discuss strategy with respect to
litigation, the public discussion of which may have a detrimental effect on the litigating
position of the Board. Votes may be taken, and if they are votes will be released at a time
deemed appropriate by counsel. The Board will not reconvene in open session. On a
polled vote the Board voted as follows: Mr. Grader, in favor; Mr. Christensen, in favor;
Mrs. Jacobi, in favor; Mr. Nye, in favor; Mrs. Belf-Becker, in favor.

6:20 p.m. the Board adjourned to Executive Session.

                                     Jason Silva
                                     Town Administrator
