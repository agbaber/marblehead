                             BOARD OF SELECTMEN

                                      MINUTES

                                    April 20, 2021

Board met in session at 7:00 p.m. via ZOOM Conferencing:

                               Jackie Belf-Becker, Chair
                                  M. C. Moses Grader
                                     James E. Nye

                           Jason Silva, Town Administrator

The Chair called the meeting of Wednesday, April 20, 2021 to order. The following roll
call was taken: Mr. Grader, present; Mr. Nye, present; Mrs. Belf-Becker, present.

BUDGETS. Selectmen’s. Revise. Motion made and seconded to approve the revised
FY22 Selectmen’s Budge for a total operating budget of $2,119,321.28. Moved by Mr.
Nye, seconded by Mr. Grader. On a polled vote the Board voted as follows: Mr. Grader,
in favor; Mr. Nye, in favor; Mrs. Belf-Becker, in favor.

The Board remained in attendance at the Finance Committee Warrant Hearing.

Motion made and seconded to adjourn at 8:59 p.m. Moved by Mr. Nye, seconded by Mr.
Grader. On a polled vote the Board voted as follows: Mr. Grader, in favor; Mr. Nye, in
favor; Mrs. Belf-Becker, in favor.
