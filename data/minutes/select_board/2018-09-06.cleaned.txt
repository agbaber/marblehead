                             BOARD OF SELECTMEN

                                     MINUTES

                                 September 6, 2018

Board met in session at 8:30 a.m. in the Selectmen’s Meeting Room at Abbot Hall
Present (constituting a quorum):

                              Jackie Belf-Becker, Chair
                               Harry C. Christensen, Jr.
                                 M. C. Moses Grader
                                   Judith R. Jacobi
                                    James E. Nye

                           Jason Silva, Town Administrator

CONTRACTS. Charles Quigley, Town Engineer, appeared before the Board to discuss
and answer questions on two bid results for seawall repairs.

CONTRACT. Grace Oliver’s Beach Seawall. Motion made and seconded to award
the contract for the Grace Oliver’s Beach Seawall project to T-Ford Company Inc. of
Georgetown, MA in the amount of ninety four thousand and fifty dollars ($94,050.00)
and authorize the Chair to sign a contract on behalf of the Board. All in favor.

CONTRACT. Front Street Seawall Repair. Motion made and seconded to reject all
bids received for the Front Street Sewall Repair Project. All in favor.

Motion made and seconded to adjourn at 9:05 a.m. All in favor.
