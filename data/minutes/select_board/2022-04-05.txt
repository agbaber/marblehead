                              BOARD OF SELECTMEN

                                      MINUTES

                                     April 5, 2022

Board met in session at 8:30 a.m. via ZOOM Conferencing
Present (constituting a quorum):

                               Jackie Belf-Becker, Chair
                                  M. C. Moses Grader
                                     James E. Nye
                                   Erin M. Noonan
                                    Alexa J. Singer

                      John J. McGinn, Interim Town Administrator

The Chair called the meeting of April 5, 2022 to order. All members of the Board are
participating remotely. The following roll call was taken: Mrs. Noonan, present; Mr.
Grader, present; Mrs. Singer, present; Mr. Nye, present; Mrs. Belf-Becker, present.

CONTRACT. State Street Restroom. MJS Construction. Motion made and
seconded to award the contract for the State Street Restroom Repairs and Renovation
project to MJS Construction of Danvers, MA in the amount of thirty-one thousand two
hundred and ninety-six dollars and authorize the Chair to sign the contract on behalf of
the Board. Moved by Mr. Nye, seconded by Mr. Grader. On a polled vote the Board
voted as follows: Mrs. Singer, in favor; Mrs. Noonan, in favor; Mr. Grader, in favor; Mr.
Nye, in favor; Mrs. Belf-Becker, in favor.

Motion made and seconded to adjourn at 8:35 a.m. Moved by Mr. Nye, seconded by Mr.
Grader. On a polled vote the Board voted as follows: Mrs. Singer, in favor; Mrs.
Noonan, in favor; Mr. Grader, in favor; Mr. Nye, in favor; Mrs. Belf-Becker, in favor.
