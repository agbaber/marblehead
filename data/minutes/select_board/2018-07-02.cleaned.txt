MARBLEHEAD BREWING CO. LLC. Father Andrew Bushell, Saint Nicholas
Shrine, 124 Pleasant Street and Cameron F. Kerry, Sidley Austin LLP, 60 State Street,
Boston, representing Saint Nicholas Shrine, appeared before the Board to discuss the
recently approved application from Marblehead Brewing Co., LLC, 124 Pleasant Street,
to alter/extend the premises. Attorney Jay Talerman, Mead, Talerman and Costa, LLC,
30 Green Street, Newburyport, was present representing the Town. Father Andrew
addressed the Board regarding the process taken at a public hearing on the application to
alter/extend the premises, from Marblehead Brewing Co., LLC, 124 Pleasant Street, in
determining the seating capacity on the submitted application. The Board explained their
decision. Father Andrew requested an opportunity to request an amendment to the
Boards decision by increasing the seating capacity from what was approved. The Board
advised Father Andrew that he should submit a request in writing to the Board and they
would hear this request at a future Selectmen’s meeting.

Motion made and seconded to adjourn at 10:15 a.m.
