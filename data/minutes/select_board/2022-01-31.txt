                              BOARD OF SELECTMEN

                                      MINUTES

                                   January 31, 2022

Board met in session at 9:30 a.m. via ZOOM Conferencing
Present (constituting a quorum):

                               Jackie Belf-Becker, Chair
                                  M. C. Moses Grader
                                     James E. Nye
                                   Erin M. Noonan
                                    Alexa J. Singer

                     John J. McGinn, Interim Town Administrator

The Chair called the meeting of January 31 2022 to order. The following roll call was
taken: Mrs. Noonan, present; Mr. Grader, present; Mrs. Singer, present; Mr. Nye,
present; Mrs. Belf-Becker, present.

SNOW EMERGENCY. Motion made and seconded that the Board of Selectmen
declare a state of emergency in the Town of Marblehead from Monday, January 31, 2022
through Wednesday, February 2, 2022 at 11:59 p.m. due to significant snow
accumulation which resulted from the snow storm of January 29, 2022 and that said snow
poses an immediate threat to the health or safety of persons or property in the Town of
Marblehead. Moved by Mr. Nye, seconded by Mr. Grader. On a polled vote the Board
voted as follows: Mrs. Noonan, in favor; Mrs. Singer, in favor; Mr. Grader, in favor; Mr.
Nye, in favor; Mrs. Belf-Becker, in favor.

Motion made and seconded to adjourn at 9:40 a.m. Moved by Mr. Nye, seconded by Mr.
Grader. On a polled vote the Board voted as follows: Mrs. Noonan, in favor; Mrs.
Singer, in favor; Mr. Grader, in favor; Mr. Nye, in favor; Mrs. Belf-Becker, in favor.
