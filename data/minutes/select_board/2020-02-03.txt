DEPARTMENT OF PUBLIC WORKS. Director. Appointment. Robert Dever
appeared before the Board. Motion made and seconded to appoint Robert Dever as
Director, Department of Public Works, effective February 10, 2020. All in favor.

LICENSING. One Day Liquor License. Star of Sea. Motion made and seconded to
approve the request from Robert Mitchell, Facilities Manager, Our Lady, Star of the Sea
Parish, for a 1 Day Liquor License on Friday, February 7, 2020 from 6:00 p.m. – 11:00
p.m. at 85 Atlantic Avenue subject to the following:
    1. Delivery of and receipt by the Licensing Authority of the required fee ($50.)
    2. Delivery of and receipt by the Licensing Authority of Proof that the alcohol will
        be purchased from an authorized source.
    3. Proof that the applicant can receive proper delivery; provide proper storage and
        disposal of all alcoholic beverages purchased all in accordance with the
        requirements of G. L. c. 138.
Alcohol will be purchased from Merrimack Valley Distributing Co., Danvers. On a
polled vote the Board voted as follows: Mr. Christensen, in favor; Mr. Nye, in favor; Mr.
Grader, in favor; Mrs. Jacobi, in favor; Mrs. Belf-Becker, in favor.
