LICENSING. Common Victualler. Eat Well Kitchen. 12 Atlantic Avenue. Susan
Bergeron, 4 Holly Circle, Swampscott, appeared before the Board seeking a Common
Victualler License for Eat Well Kitchen. Motion made and seconded to approve the
application from Eat Well Kitchen, for a Common Victualler License at 12 Atlantic
Avenue, hours of operation Monday – Sunday 7:00 am – 10:00 pm, subject to receipt of
the required fees, forms and receipt of all inspectional sign offs. Seating capacity is 20.
All in favor.

Motion made and seconded to adjourn at 3:35 p.m. All in favor.
