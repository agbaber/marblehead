                              BOARD OF SELECTMEN

                                       MINUTES

                                   January 21, 2021

Board met in session at 1:30 p.m. via ZOOM Conferencing:

                                Jackie Belf-Becker, Chair
                                   M. C. Moses Grader
                                      James E. Nye

                            Jason Silva, Town Administrator

The Chair called the meeting of Thursday, January 21, 2021 to order. The following roll
call was taken: Mr. Grader, present; Mr. Nye, present; Mrs. Belf-Becker, present.

PERMISSION. Block the Public Way. Atlantic and Chestnut. Commercial Shoot.
Jeff McLean appeared before the Board. Motion made and seconded to approve the
request from Jeff MacLean, Location Manager, HeyLetsGo!, to block 6 parking spaces
on Atlantic Avenue and corner of Chestnut Street, on Monday, January 25, 2021 from
7:00 am – 7:00 pm, rain/snow date of Tuesday, January 26, 2021, to film a video for
Simply Business. Approval is subject to receipt of the required Certificate of Insurance,
Police detail and adherence to all current state and local COVID-10 safety protocols.
Traffic shall not be impeded. Moved by Mr. Grader, seconded by Mr. Nye. On a polled
vote the Board voted as follows: Mr. Grader, in favor; Mr. Nye, in favor; Mrs. Belf-
Becker, in favor.

Motion made and seconded to adjourn 1:35 p.m. Moved by Mr. Nye, seconded by Mr.
Grader. On a polled vote the Board voted as follows: Mr. Grader, in favor; Mr. Nye, in
favor; Mrs. Belf-Becker, in favor.
