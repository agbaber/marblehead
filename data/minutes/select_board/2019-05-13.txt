REMOTE PARTICIPATION POLICY. The Board received for review a draft
Remote Participation Policy for the Town of Marblehead. Motion made and seconded to
review the Remote Participation Policy and vote on it at their next meeting. All in favor.

ELECTIONS.
Moved and seconded: that prior public notice of the Board of Selectmen’s intention to
call a Special Town Meeting for the purpose of a Special Election for approval of debt
exclusions under Proposition 2 ½ be waived because in the opinion of the Board of
Selectmen the public interest would suffer from such delay. All in favor.

Moved and seconded: that a Special Town Meeting be called for June 18, 2019, for the
purpose of a Special Election to vote on questions relative to debt exclusions under
Proposition 2 ½. All in favor.

Moved and seconded: that the Warrant be opened for insertion of questions. All in favor.
