                                         SELECT BOARD

                                             MINUTES

                                          October 1, 2025

Board met in HYBRID session at 4:15 p.m. at Abbot Hall, 188 Washington Street, Select
Board Meeting Room

The following Board members were present constituting a quorum:

                                         Dan Fox, Chair
                                        M. C. Moses Grader
                                         Erin M. Noonan
                                          Alexa J. Singer
                                         James R. Zisson

                          Thatcher W. Kezer III, Town Administrator

The Chair called the meeting of October 1, 2025, to order.

EXECUTIVE SESSION. Motion to adjourn to Executive Session under M.G.L. c. 30A,
Sec. 21 for the purpose of discussing pending litigation where the public discussion on
the same may have a detrimental effect on the negotiating position of the public body,
votes may be taken, and if they are votes will be released at a time deemed appropriate by
counsel. The board will not reconvene in open session. Moved by Mr. Grader, seconded
by Mrs. Singer. On a polled vote the Board voted as follows: Mrs. Noonan, in favor;
Mr. Grader, in favor; Mrs. Singer, in favor; Mr. Zisson, in favor; Mr. Fox, in favor.

The Board adjourned to Executive Session.
