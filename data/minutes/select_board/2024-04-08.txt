                                     SELECT BOARD

                                         MINUTES

                                        April 8, 2024

Board met in HYBRID session at 7:00 p.m. at Abbot Hall, 188 Washington Street
The following Board members were present constituting a quorum:

                                   Erin M. Noonan, Chair
                                    M. C. Moses Grader
                                       Bret T. Murray
                                        James E. Nye
                                       Alexa J. Singer

                          Thatcher W. Kezer III, Town Administrator

The Chair called the meeting of April 8, 2024 to order.

FY25 BUDGET. A motion was made and seconded to reduce Other General
Government by $250,000 to $21,198,507. This motion will reduce funding OPEB line
item by $250,000 to make funds available for other appropriations. This reduction will
not have a material impact on the OPEB liability in the short term. The Board discussed
further. Mr. Murray asked where these funds would specifically be going, and the Town
Administrator noted they would be available for the school Budget. Other Board
members expressed their concern regarding this request and the additional request for
funding from the schools. 4 voted in favor, Mr. Murray voted opposed.

The Board continued their meeting attending the Finance Committee Warrant Hearing.

Motion made and seconded to adjourn at 11:20 p.m. All in favor.
