VETERANS AGENT. Memorial Day. David Rodgers, Veterans’ Agent, appeared
before the Board seeking approval for Memorial Day events. Due to COVID-19 the
Town announced the following Memorial Day events:
Saturday, May 29, 2021. 9:00 a.m. from the Bell Tower at Waterside Cemetery.
Veteran’s Grave Flagging. All are welcome to participate in honoring Marblehead’s
Veterans. Anyone needing a flag for a Veteran’s Grave may call the Veterans Office at
781-631-0990.
Sunday, May 30, 2021. 1:00 p.m. Small ceremony at Star of the Sea Cemetery by the
flagpole.
Monday, May 31, 2021. Traditional Memorial Day service at Memorial Park with
speakers.
Motion made and seconded to approve the Memorial Day events as presented subject to
guidance from the state. Moved by Mr. Grader, seconded by Mr. Nye. On a polled vote
the Board voted as follows: Mr. Nye, in favor; Mr. Grader, in favor; Mrs. Belf-Becker, in

ARO CANNABIS CO. Presentation. Mark Shuparra, Aro Cannabis Co., and Phil
Silverman, Counsel, appeared before the Board to present an application to locate a
marijuana retail establishment in the Town of Marblehead at 31 Atlantic Avenue. In
accordance with state requirements, Aro Cannabis hosted a virtual Community Outreach
Meeting on March 10, 2021, to allow the public to learn about their proposal, ask
questions and offer feedback. The next step in the approval process is for the Town to
enter into a Host Community Agreement with Aro Cannabis. Before the Board of
Selectmen consider this request, and to ensure residents have ample opportunity to
provide input, the Board of Selectmen will open a 30-day comment period on the
proposal from Aro Cannabis. You can view the presentation that Aro Cannabis provided
to the Board of Selectmen at www.marblehead.org. If you would like to offer any
feedback on the Aro Cannabis proposal, please email Kyle Wiley in the Selectmen’s
Office at wileyk@marblehead.org. The comment period will be open until Monday, May
17, 2021.
