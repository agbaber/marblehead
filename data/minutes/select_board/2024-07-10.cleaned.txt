GIFT TO TOWN. Portrait. Sally and Larry Sands appeared before the Board seeking
to donate to the Town a portrait of Sally’s great grandfather, Henry Florence Pitman,
born in 1808 and died in 1885. He was a trustee of the Abbot Fund and the building
committee, and his name is on the plaque outside the Select Board’s office. He lived on
Pleasant Street and a book has been written on the house that features a chapter about
Henry Pitman, known as the 5th wealthiest person in the town at the time owning vast
amounts of property throughout town. Motion made and seconded to accept, as a gift to
the Town, a portrait of Henry Florence Pitman, and put in the care and custody of the
Historical Commission. All in favor.

DISCUSSION. Harbors and Waters Board/Zoning Board of Appeals.
Harbors and Waters Board. The Board discussed annual re-appointments to the
various Boards and Committee as it relates to possible reorganization of those boards.
Mrs. Noonan said that both herself and Mr. Grader spoke with Gary Gregory, Chair of
Harbors and Waters and said he was in favor of increasing the membership. The Board
discussed various options related to membership numbers, composition and terms based
on having looked at other towns to see how their Harbors and Waters Board are formed.
The Board would like to review the Mission of the Harbors and Waters to better represent
its current activities. The Chair will work with Gary Gregory and Town Administrator
on a draft updated statement to bring to the Board at their next meeting. The following
votes were taken:

Motion made and seconded to amend the Harbors and Waters Board from 5 Member/3
Alternates Board to 7 Members/0 Alternates to be effective upon the reappointment of
the membership of the new board at a future meeting. All in favor.

Motion to amend the terms of the Harbors and Waters Board from 1 year to 3-year
staggered terms as follows: 3 members for 3 year terms; 2 members for 2 year terms; 2
members for 1 year terms; effective upon the reappointment of the membership of the
new board at a future meeting. All in favor.

Letters of Interest. The Board will receive letters of interest to serve on the Harbors and
Waters Board through August 9, 2024 with interviews and appointments on August 14,
2024. Send letter of interest to the Select Board, Abbot Hall, 188 Washington Street, or
email wileyk@marblehead.org.

Zoning Board of Appeals. Mr. Fox spoke with the ZBA Chair regarding the
composition of the ZBA, which is required, and is good with moving forward with
reappointment of the ZBA.
