                              BOARD OF SELECTMEN

                                       MINUTES

                                     May 17, 2021

Board met in session at 10:00 a.m. via ZOOM Conferencing:

                                Jackie Belf-Becker, Chair
                                   M. C. Moses Grader
                                      James E. Nye

                            Jason Silva, Town Administrator

The Chair called the meeting of Monday, May 17, 2021 to order. The following roll call
was taken: Mr. Grader, present; Mr. Nye, present; Mrs. Belf-Becker, present.

ELECTIONS. Motion made and seconded that the Board of Selectmen rescind the vote
of May 7, 2021, calling a Special Election for the purpose of approval of debt exclusions
under Proposition 2 ½ and that in its stead the following questions be added to the
Annual Town Election ballot to be held on June 22, 2021. Moved by Mr. Nye, seconded
by Mr. Grader. On a polled vote the Board voted as follows: Mr. Grader, in favor; Mr.
Nye, in favor; Mrs. Belf-Becker, in favor.

MOTION OF THE BOARD OF SELECTMEN BY TWO-THIRDS VOTE TO SEEK
VOTER APPROVAL AT THE ANNUAL TOWN ELECTION TO EXCLUDE FROM
THE PROVISIONS OF GENERAL LAWS CHAPTER 59, SECTION 21C, THE
FUNDS REQUIRED FOR THE PAYMENT OF PRINCIPAL AND INTEREST ON
BONDS, NOTE OR CERTIFICATIONS OF INDEBTEDNESS.

Motion made and seconded that the Board of Selectmen seek voter approval at the
ANNUAL TOWN Election to be held on June 22, 2021, to exempt from the provisions of
General Laws Chapter 59, Section 21C, the funds required for the payment of principal
and interest on bonds, notes, or certificates of indebtedness, all as set forth in the
following Question 1:

Question 1: Shall the Town of Marblehead be allowed to exempt from the provisions of
proposition two and one-half, so-called, the amounts required to pay for the bond issued
in order to pay costs of purchasing and equipping a new pumper truck for the Fire
Department, and for the payment of all other costs incidental and related thereto?
YES_________           NO_________
Moved by Mr. Nye, seconded by Mr. Grader. On a polled vote the Board voted as
follows: Mr. Grader, in favor; Mr. Nye, in favor; Mrs. Belf-Becker, in favor.
