                             BOARD OF SELECTMEN

                                     MINUTES

                                     May 3, 2021

Board met in session at 6:45 p.m. 80 Atlantic Avenue, Star of the Sea Community
Center, Rear Parking Lot.

                               Jackie Belf-Becker, Chair
                                  M. C. Moses Grader
                                     James E. Nye

                           Jason Silva, Town Administrator

The Chair called the meeting of Monday, May 3, 2021 to order. Mr. Grader, Mr. Nye
and Mrs. Belf-Becker were present.

WARRANTS ARTICLES. 2021 Annual Town Meeting. The Board reviewed articles
they have sponsored on the 2021 Town Warrant and the following votes were taken:
Motion made and seconded to approve Article 20 Collective Bargaining (Fire) and the
Memorandum of Understanding (MOU), dated May 3, 2021, between the Town of
Marblehead and the International Association of Firefighters, AFL-CIO, CLC and Local
2043 as prepared by Assistant Town Counsel Marc J. Miller. All in favor.

Motion made and seconded to indefinitely postpone Article 21, Collective Bargaining
(Police) and Article 22, Collective Bargaining (IUE/CWA – Local 1776). All in favor.

Motion made and seconded to approve Article 25 Proposed Reclassification and Pay
Schedule (Administrative) as presented, Article 28 Compensation – Town Officers as
presented and Article 29 Ratification of Salary Bylaw as presented. All in favor.

Motion made and seconded to Indefinitely Postpone Article 44, Compensation. All in
favor.

2021 Annual Town Meeting commenced.

Motion made and seconded to adjourn at 11:00 p.m. All in favor.
