                              BOARD OF SELECTMEN

                                       MINUTES

                                     April 30, 2021

Board met in session at 10:00 a.m. via ZOOM Conferencing:

                                Jackie Belf-Becker, Chair
                                   M. C. Moses Grader
                                      James E. Nye

                            Jason Silva, Town Administrator

The Chair called the meeting of Friday, April 30, 2021 to order. The following roll call
was taken: Mr. Grader, present; Mr. Nye, present; Mrs. Belf-Becker, present.

ARBOR DAY PROCLAMATION. Motion made and seconded to approve the request
from Jonathan Fobert, Tree Warden, to proclaim Friday, April 30, 2021 as Arbor Day for
the Town of Marblehead. 100 Common Lilac seedlings have been ordered and will be
planted by the Conservancy in the conservation areas, trees were planted on Countryside
Lane, Leo Road and Birch Street in association with Sustainable Marblehead and Iron
Tree Services donated pruning services at Glabicky Green in honor of Arbor day. Moved
by Mr. Nye, seconded by Mr. Grader. On a polled vote the Board voted as follows: Mr.
Grader, in favor; Mr. Nye, in favor; Mrs. Belf-Becker, in favor.

Motion made and seconded to adjourn at 10:05 a.m. Moved by Mr. Nye, seconded by
Mr. Grader. On a polled vote the Board voted as follows: Mr. Grader, in favor; Mr. Nye,
in favor; Mrs. Belf-Becker, in favor.
