The Chair called the meeting of Tuesday, March 17, 2020 to order. On a roll call vote by
the Town Administrator the following Board members participated in this meeting: Mr.
Christensen, present; Mr. Grader, remotely; Mr. Nye, remotely; Mrs. Jacobi, remotely;
Mrs. Belf-Becker, remotely.

OVERNIGH PARKING BAN. Lift. Motion made and seconded to lift the Overnight
parking Ban on Wednesday, March 18, 2020 at 12:01 A.M. In the event of a snow
emergency prior to April 15, 2020 all cars will be required to be off the public way and
the winter parking ban would be in effect immediately until further notice. This
information will be posted on the towns’ website, www.marblehead.org. On a polled
vote the Board voted as follows: Mr. Christensen, in favor; Mr. Jacobi, in favor; Mr.
Grader, in favor; Mr. Nye, in favor; Mrs. Belf-Becker, in favor.

Motion made and seconded to adjourn at 8:35 a.m. On a polled vote the Board voted as
follows: Mr. Christensen, in favor; Mr. Jacobi, in favor; Mr. Grader, in favor; Mr. Nye,
in favor; Mrs. Belf-Becker, in favor.
