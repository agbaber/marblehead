- **"Substantive discussion" threshold is subjective.** Several budget presentations are single-sentence mentions ("appeared before the Board to present") with no recorded deliberation. I treated the vote itself as the attempt when the underlying line item is systemic, even without visible debate in the minutes. A stricter reading would require recorded discussion beyond the motion.
